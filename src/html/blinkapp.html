ABOUT BLINK ANDROID APP

The Blink Android app had always been lacking behind it's iOS counterpart. So while interning for Blink in the summer of 2017, I helped with an almost complete re-write of the app.
I also had the chance to work with Blink's internal APIs and understand how the system operated, allowing me to make a dashboard presenting valuable information on top of my cubicle for everyone to see.

This was my first internship and my first introduction to software engineering in a corporate environment.
Over the summer, I gained a lot of experience with Android development. Previously I had only worked on Android apps as a hobby. View the App on the Play Store.
